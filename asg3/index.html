    Background

    This organization was founded by J Alejandro Gonzalez who was diagnosed with Autism at a young age and growing up he always had some sturggles with understanding lessons at school and communicating with people. But most importantly if it wasn't for his hard working parents saving paycheck after paycheck to get him a higher education along with material he may need.     Raise enough money to help many young adults on the spectrum with affording to attend college, job opportunities, and getting ready for adult life.

    Target Audience

    Young Adults and children on the spectrum who can't afford a normal or higher education and require certain needs in order to attend school.
