DINING

Hungry for some good sweets or fine cuisine? Well, check out some of our amazing restaurants, all of which serve a variety of good food for anyone to enjoy!!

Sugar Cube Corner

Owned by the lovely Cake family, Sugarcube Corner is known for being the number one place for ponies and creatures alike to get different pastries and desserts. Ranging from cupcakes, birthday cakes, muffins, doughnuts, and many more!!

The Tasty Treat

Located in the Restaurant Row with some of Canterlot's well-reviewed eating establishments. The Tasty Treat serves some great dishes from Indian culture. It used to be very underrated until Pinkie Pie and Rarity would help the owners gain more attention and beat out the so-called critics.

The Hay Burger

Or maybe you prefer something more laidback and simple, similar to let's say "fast food". Well then, hayburger is just for you! Dine in on some well-crafted burgers, grilled cheese, onion rings, mozzarella sticks, and many more, all at an affordable cost!
